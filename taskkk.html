Cheese cake

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert loved worldwide for its velvety texture and luxurious flavor. It is typically made with a combination of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, set atop a crust made from crushed graham crackers, cookies, or sponge cake.

Macrons

Macarons are a popular and elegant French confection known for their delicate texture and vibrant appearance. They are made with almond flour, egg whites, and sugar, resulting in a smooth, crisp shell that gives way to a soft and chewy interior.

Stacked Brownies

Stacked Brownies are a delightful dessert variation where layers of rich, fudgy brownies are stacked, often with additional ingredients or flavors between the layers to enhance the treat. They are both visually appealing and indulgently delicious.

Cupcakes

Cupcakes are small, individual-sized cakes baked in a cup-shaped mold or liner, typically made of paper or foil. They are a popular dessert due to their convenient size, versatility, and the ability to customize flavors, toppings, and decorations.
